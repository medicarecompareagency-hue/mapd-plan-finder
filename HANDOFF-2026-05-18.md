## Lessons captured this session
- "Skipped pipeline step" looks identical to "broken classifier" from
  outside — always check that ALL pipeline steps ran, not just whether
  the first one's output looks right
- Neon pooler has silent cold-start (~3 min) on first big write — not
  hung, just patient
- PowerShell strips `$` from inline node -e — escape with backtick:
  `p.\`$disconnect()`
- Read-Host silently accepts empty input; safer to use literal
  `$env:FOO = 'value'` assignments
- enrich-* script flag semantics differ — most need `--apply`, but
  partial-dual-coinsurance defaults to APPLY with `--dry-run` to dry
